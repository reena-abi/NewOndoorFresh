Ondoorfresh Meat Export India Ltd

Ondoorfresh Meat Export India Ltd. is a one-stop destination for farmers and entrepreneur. It was established in the year 2020 with a good vision to buy varieties of quality products like raw crops, fruits, vegetables from agricultural farmers, milk from dairy farms, goats, hens from animal husbandry farmers, and fish from aqua farmers and provide best price to farmers for their products according to market.

Every type of farmer Large as well as small scale can join us for short or long term and sell their product on our platform.

Company reaches out its products to the consumers and keeping this in mind the company has a senior team related to agriculture sector whose main work is to encourage those farmers who have joined hands with Ondoorfresh Meat Export India Ltd., to start organic farming so that farmers can yield better quality crops in quantity. Company does not demand any extra charges; this is a small contribution of our company for farmers of India. This will benefit both farmers as well as consumers by providing them with best quality products.

Company has its own supply chain to reach out its products to wholesalers, vendors, caterers, retailers, franchise partner and consumers.

Our VISION

To bring impactful changes in the life of farmers, by significantly increasing their income, thereby health, standard of living and social status as well. And our aim is to join 1 crore farmers in our platform within next 5 years.
